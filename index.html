Hi and welcome!

I Love Design

My name is Brenda Flores. I attended the Design Institute of San Diego, and majored in Fine Arts with the sole emphasis in Interior Design. After Graduating in 2015, I enhanced my skills with local and reputable design firms. My experiences vary from learning the key elements to working with clients, to creating construction documents for major hotel brands. Today, I continue to enhance my knowledge and skillset in the hospitality sector within the design community.
